2. ならう(習う)  /   まなぶ (学ぶ)  /  べんきょう(勉強)
narau vs manabu vs benkyou

narau, manabu = learn
benkyousuru = study

"narau" has passive meaning (at school or class).
the others has active meaning (learn by myself).
