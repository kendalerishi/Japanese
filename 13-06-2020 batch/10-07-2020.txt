1. What is the difference between かなり and とても 

Well, it's here. Please have a look. It's too simple.

かなり means quite, but とても means really/very. とても is stronger than かなり.

For example:
①この かばんは かなり重いですよ。「the bag is quite heavy」

②この かばんは とても重いですよ。「the bag is really/ very heavy」

Easy right ?


2. 先生(せんせい) Vs 教師(きょうし)

Both mean 'Teacher'. But there's a difference. ??

Other than 'teacher' , we use the word 先生 for someone who guides us .
It can be used for many professions like doctor , lawyer , teacher etc. While , 教師 strictly means a 'teacher'.

So , all 教師 can be called 先生 but not all 先生 are 教師.
